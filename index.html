we're kayla and ryan. we're inspired by the sea and different parts of our earth. we've loved each other for half of our lives and now share our love with our puppy, joe, and our new project, solas.
solas means light in irish and may be interpreted as both solace and illumination.

we source sculptural vessels that were loved in a past life in sydney and beyond. we repurpose them with a unique coating in earthy tones and textures and sometimes add brass light fixtures and rattan shades.
no two are the same.
